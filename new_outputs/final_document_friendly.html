1.1 DOCUMENT CONTROL PROCEDURE (Last Updated 04/12/2024) (Revision 15)

E007

1.1.1 Purpose

E008

The purpose of this procedure is to define how controlled documents are:
- approved and authorised for issue
- distributed
- reviewed.

E009

1.2 Application

E010

This procedure applies to all documents used to define or referred to by the DANAOS SAFETY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM. All such documents are considered as controlled documents. These documents are the following:
* Policy Manual
* Describes the company's 'Safety & Environmental Protection Policy' & provides the framework upon which the company's 'Safety Management System' is established.
* Procedures
* Describes the basic Company Procedures covering shipboard operations.
* Emergency Procedures Manual
* Fleet Instructions Manual
* SOPEP
* SOLAS FIRE TRAINING MANUAL
* GARBAGE MANAGEMENT PLAN MANUAL
* BALLAST WATER MANAGEMENT PLAN
* CYBER SECURITY MANUAL
* VECTOR MANAGEMENT PLAN
* ECDIS MANAGEMENT MANUAL
* INVENTORY OF HAZARDOUS MATERIAL
* MOORING SYSTEM MANAGEMENT PLAN (MSMP)

E011

Furthermore, a number of reference publications (vessel & office library) and all Records (Forms) & Checklists are also controlled through this procedure.

1.3.3 Reviews & Amendments To Controlled Documents

E037

Reviews to controlled documents are approved by the same persons as for their original issue (section 3.1).

E038

Necessity of revision derives from Management reviews, any CHANGE REQUEST form issued or Dept. Director requirement for amendments to procedures affecting his Dept. Any amendment is screened for ISM, ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and ISO 50001 compliance by the SQE Director and thereafter is forwarded to the Management for approval. Once approval is granted, the SQE coordinator updates ISM administrator with the new revision and distributes as per below distribution list.

E039

Procedures shall be revised & issued to the next revision number as a whole document. As far as the rest of the Controlled Documents is concerned, revisions shall be carried out on a section by section basis. Amendments will be in Blue and italics font style.

E040

Whenever there is an amendment to controlled documents, all obsolete version(s) will be removed from areas of work to prevent the unintended use. DSMS documentation (Emergency Procedures Manual, Policy Manual, Procedures Manual, Fleet Instructions Manual) will be updated through ISM Administrator and a hard copy will also remain on board in Ship Office and Bridge in accordance with Filing System (P21). The SQE Coordinator is also responsible to forward amended Controlled Document to the vessels, accompanied by the DTF, which will be signed by the Master and returned to Head Office (one copy kept on board). The Master is responsible to remove the obsolete documents from the area of work, keeping them (marked Superseded) in his Superseded Document file.

E041

Controlled documents are kept within Company ISM administrator and updated through this system. For reference purposes, through the Administrator tools, the list of updated versions are printed and sent onboard, originally, and thereafter whenever there is a change of documentation involved.

E042

1.3.4 Reference Publication

E043

In support of Controlled Documents, the Company supplies reference publication (e.g. SOLAS, MARPOL, STCW, etc.) in their latest edition for the instruction and guidance of Masters, Officers, crew and shore based staff. Such documents are kept in the Head Office library and in a library established on board each vessel. A register of such reference documentation is kept, indicating also their latest edition.

E044

Documents included in the vessels and office library are listed in the 'Library List'.

E045

The SQE Coordinator is responsible to ensure that all reference publications are updated to the latest edition ashore and Operations Dept. is responsible for updating the vessel 'Library List'.

E046

The Master is responsible to ensure that the on board Library List is kept updated at all times and keeps Danaos Ops advised in case of any requirement.
